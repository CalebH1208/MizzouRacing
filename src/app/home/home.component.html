About Us


Mizzou Racing is the University of Missouri’s Formula SAE Team.

We build a ¼ scale formula style car each year and compete against universities from around the world. The car is designed, manufactured, and built by our members; our organization is entirely student run. We have five sub teams: aerodynamics, electronics, powertrain/drivetrain, suspension, and brakes & controls. Our team is made up of engineering students (as well as other majors) ranging from freshmen to seniors. We compete in the combustion class of FSAE.
