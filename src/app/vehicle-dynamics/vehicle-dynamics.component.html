Vehicle Dynamics

What We Do...

Our suspension and chassis subteams make up the vehicle dynamics team. We design and manufacture components in house with our manual and CNC machines. Some highlights of our suspension package are KW V5 dampers, adjustable anti-roll bars, and carbon fiber rods. The team adjusts the toe and camber for events to maximize performance through extensive testing.

Sub Team Lead: Aaron Jewett

Aaron is a senior mechanical engineering major. He joined the team in the fall of 2021, and served in roles such as Brakes & Controls lead, and president. Aaron has been studying suspension geometry and chassis design while updating the components on the cars. His goals for the year inculde a rear strucutre update, upright redesign, tire models, and data acquistion to make on track adjustments.
