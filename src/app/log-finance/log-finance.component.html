Logistics and Finance

What We Do...

We keep the team running. Ensuring deadlines are hit and budgets are balanced. The ability to make it to competition each year necessitates organization, fiscal responsibility, and communication. Nobody wants to maintain the spreadsheets... but someone has to.

Sub Team Lead: Justin Bowers

Justin is a third-year Computer Science and Electrical Engineering student, and the President of Mizzou Racing. He joined the team in Fall 2022 after he saw a flyer hanging outside of Naka Hall. Justin was the team’s treasurer in 2023-24. He designs electrical harnesses and embedded software systems, and considers himself the “resident CAN (theory) expert.” Justins goals for the year are to end the year with money in the bank, and exist through the year.

“Frugality is a virtue; that we have yet to learn.”

- Justin Bowers
